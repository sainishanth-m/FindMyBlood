A donor’s location becomes visible only when they manually turn on their status as an “Active Donor.” This means donors have complete control over when they appear on the map. As soon as a donor switches their status to inactive, their location is immediately hidden. This ensures privacy for donors and accuracy for patients, so you only see people who are genuinely available to help at that moment.

Once a suitable donor is found, patients can contact them directly through the app using the provided call or in-app communication options. This allows patients to quickly explain the emergency, confirm availability, and coordinate hospital details without delays. The contact process is kept simple and direct so that critical time is not wasted during urgent situations.

No. FindMyBlood is completely free for both patients and donors. There are no subscription fees, hidden charges, or commissions involved. The platform was built with the sole purpose of helping people find blood quickly during emergencies, and we believe that saving lives should never depend on money.

Generally, anyone between 18 and 65 years of age who meets local health guidelines can register as a donor on the platform. Donors are encouraged to be honest about their health and availability. Final eligibility for blood donation is always determined by the hospital or blood bank, and patients should rely on hospital screening for medical confirmation before donation.

FindMyBlood uses real-time technology to help patients locate the nearest available donors as quickly as possible. The system identifies active donors based on blood group and location and updates results instantly. This reduces the time spent searching manually and helps patients connect with nearby donors without delays, which can be crucial in emergency situations.

If a donor is unavailable to donate at a particular time, they can simply change their status to inactive. Once this is done, they will no longer appear in patient search results. This ensures that patients only see donors who are currently willing and able to help, reducing confusion and unanswered calls during emergencies.

Donors are encouraged to keep their profiles updated with correct blood group details and availability. While FindMyBlood provides a fast connection between patients and donors, patients are advised to verify identity and blood group at the hospital. Medical verification and screening are always handled by healthcare professionals to ensure safety.

Yes. Data security is taken very seriously. Personal information is protected using secure storage and encryption methods. Contact details are shared only when a donor agrees to be contacted, and information is never misused or shared for non-medical purposes. The platform is designed to keep both patients and donors safe and protected.

If a donor does not respond, patients can immediately contact another nearby donor shown on the map. Since multiple donors are displayed in real time, you are not dependent on just one person. This increases the chances of finding help quickly without unnecessary waiting.
