Eligibility Criteria

  • Age between 18-65 years
  • Weight above 45 kg
  • Hemoglobin level above 12.5 g/dL
  • No major illness in the past 6 months
  • Gap of 3 months since last donation

About FindMyBlood

FindMyBlood is a life-saving platform designed to instantly connect patients in need of blood with verified nearby donors. When every minute matters, FindMyBlood removes delays, confusion, and dependency on inefficient systems.
